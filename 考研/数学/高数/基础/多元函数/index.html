    多元函数

    基本概念¶

    极限¶

    连续¶

    如果\(\displaystyle\lim_{(x,y)\to(x_0,y_0)}f(x,y)= f(x_0,y_0)\)，则称\(f(x,y)\)在点\((x_0 ,y_0)\)处连续

    偏导数¶

    \(f'_x(x_0,y_0)=\displaystyle\lim_{\Delta x\to 0}\dfrac{f(x_0+\Delta x, y_0)-f(x_0,y_0)}{\Delta x}=\displaystyle\lim_{x\to x_0}\dfrac{f(x, y_0)-f(x_0,y_0)}{x-x_0}\) \(f'_y(x_0,y_0)=\displaystyle\lim_{\Delta y\to 0}\dfrac{f(x_0, y_0+\Delta y)-f(x_0,y_0)}{\Delta y}=\displaystyle\lim_{y\to y_0}\dfrac{f(x_0, y)-f(x_0,y_0)}{y-y_0}\)

    高阶偏导数¶

    判断偏导数是否连续¶

    求的点为\((x_0,y_0)\) 1. 定义法求\(f'_x(x_0,y_0),f'_y(x_0,y_0)\) 2. 公式法求\(f'_x(x,y),f'_y(x,y)\) 3. 判断是否\(\begin{cases}\displaystyle\lim_{(x,y)\to (x_0,y_0)}f'_x(x_0,y_0)=f'_x(x,y)\\\displaystyle\lim_{(x,y)\to (x_0,y_0)}f'_y(x_0,y_0)=f'_y(x,y)\end{cases}\)，若是，则\(z=f(x,y)\)在点\((x_0 ,y_0)\)处的偏导数连续

    可微¶

    全增量\(\Delta z=f(x_0+\Delta x, y_0+\Delta y)-f(x_0, y_0)=A\Delta x+B\Delta y+o(\phi)\) 全微分\(dz=A\Delta x+B\Delta y=\dfrac{\partial z}{\partial x}dx+\dfrac{\partial z}{\partial y}dy\)

    判断是否可微¶

    1. 写出全增量\(\Delta z=f(x_0+\Delta x, y_0+\Delta y)-f(x_0, y_0)\)
    2. 写出线性增量\(dz=A\Delta x+B\Delta y\),其中\(A=f'_x(x_0,y_0),B=f'_y(x_0 ,y_0)\)
    3. 若\(\displaystyle\lim_{(\Delta x,\Delta y)\to (0,0)}\dfrac{\Delta z-(A\Delta x+B\Delta y)}{\sqrt{(\Delta x)^2+(\Delta y)^2}}=0\)，则\(z=f(x,y)\)在点\((x_0,y_0)\)可微；否则，就不可微

    方向导数¶

    设三元函数\(u=u(x,y,z)\)在点\(P_{0}(x_{0},y_{0},z_{0})\)处可微分，则\(u=u(x,y,z)\)在点\(P_{0}\)处沿任一方向\(l\)的方向导数都存在，且方向导数\(\displaystyle\frac{\partial u}{\partial l}\bigg|_{p_{0}}=u'_{x}(P_{0})\cos\alpha+u'_{y}(P_{0})\cos\beta+u'_{z}(P_{0})\cos\gamma\)，cos为方向余弦

    梯度¶

    方向导数最大的方向\(quad\ u\bigg|_{P_{0}}=(u'_{x},u'_{y},u'_{z})\) 方向导数=\(quad\ u\bigg|_{P_{0}}\cdot(\cos\alpha,\cos\beta,\cos\gamma)=quad\ u\bigg|_{P_{0}}\cdot \cos\theta\)

    散度与旋度¶

    三重积分¶

    概念¶

    以\(f(x,y,z)\)为点密度的空间物体的质量

    性质¶

    普通对称性¶

    假设\(\Omega\)关于\(yOz\)面对称 \(\displaystyle\iiint\limits_{\Omega}f(x,y,z)dv=\begin{cases}2\displaystyle\iiint\limits_{\Omega_1}f(x,y,z)dv &,\ f(x,y,z)=f(-x,y,z)\\0 & ,\ f(x,y,z)=-f(-x,y,z)\end{cases}\) - 其他面对称类似

    轮换对称性¶

    若x和y对调后\(\Omega\)不变，则\(\displaystyle\iiint\limits_{\Omega}f(x,y,z)dv=\displaystyle\iiint\limits_{\Omega}f(y,x,z)dv\)

    计算¶

    直角坐标系¶

    先z后xy¶

    投影穿线，适合右上下曲面，无侧面或侧面为柱面的情况 \(\displaystyle\iiint\limits_{\Omega}f(x,y,z)dv=\iint\limits_{D_{xy}}d\sigma\int_{x_{1}(x,y)}^{x_{2}(x,y)}f(x,y,z)dz\)

    先xy后z¶

    定限截面，适合旋转体(绕z轴旋转) \(\displaystyle\iiint\limits_{\Omega}f(x,y,z)dv=\int_{a}^{b}dz\iint\limits_{D_{xy}}f(x,y,z)d\sigma\)

    柱面坐标系¶

    若先z后xy方法中的\(\displaystyle\iint\limits_{D_{xy}}d\sigma\)适用极坐标系，则令\(\displaystyle\begin{cases}x=r\cos\theta\\y=r\sin\theta\end{cases}\)，则\(\(\displaystyle\iiint\limits_{\Omega}f(x,y,z)dv=\iiint\limits_{\Omega}f(r\cos\theta,r\sin\theta,z)rdrd\theta dz\)\)

    球面坐标系¶

    当 - 被积函数有\(\displaystyle\begin{cases}f(x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2})\\f(x^{2}+y^{2})\end{cases}\) - 积分区域为 球/锥或其部分

    令\(\(\displaystyle\begin{cases}x=r\sin\varphi\cos\theta\\y=r\sin\varphi\sin\theta\\z=r\cos\varphi\end{cases}\)\) 则\(\(\displaystyle\iiint\limits_{\Omega}f(x,y,z)dxdydz=\iiint\limits_{\Omega}f(r\sin\varphi\cos\theta,r\sin\varphi\sin\theta,r\cos\varphi)r^{2}\sin\varphi d\varphi d\theta dr\)\)

    第一型曲面积分¶

    概念¶

    二重积分底为平面，第一型曲面积分底为曲面，即\(\displaystyle\iint\limits_{\Sigma}f(x,y,z)dS\)

    计算¶

    一投二代三计算 - 一投：将\(\Sigma\)投影到某一平面(如xOy)，得到投影区域\(D_{xy}\) - 二代：将\(z=z(x,y)\)或\(F(x,y,z)=0\)代入\(f(x,y,z)\) - 三计算：计算\(z'_{x}, z'_{y}\)，计算出\(dS=\sqrt{1+(z'_{x})^{2}+(z'_{y})^{2}}dxdy\)

    几何量¶

    平面¶

    • \(y=f(x), x=a, x=b, x=0\)围成的曲边梯形，面积=\(\displaystyle\int_{a}^{b}|f(x)|dx\)
    • 一般情况面积=\(\displaystyle\iint\limits_{D}d\sigma\)

    空间¶

    • D为底，曲面\(z=z(x,y)\)为顶的曲顶柱面，体积=\(\displaystyle\iint\limits_{D}|z(x,y)|d\sigma\)
    • 空间光滑曲线\(L=\begin{cases}x=x(t)\\y=y(t)\\z=z(t)\end{cases}, (\alpha\leq t\leq\beta)\)，弧长=\(\displaystyle\int_{\alpha}^{\beta}\sqrt{[x'(t)]^{2}+[y'(t)]^{2}+[z'(t)]^{2}}dt\)
    • 空间光滑曲面\(\Sigma=z=z(x,y)\)，\(D_{xy}为\Sigma在xOy面\)上的投影，面积=\(\displaystyle\iint\limits_{D_{yz}}\sqrt{1+(z'_{x})^2+(z'_{y})^2}dxdy\)
      • \(yOz\)面的投影面积=\(\displaystyle\iint\limits_{D_{xy}}\sqrt{1+(x'_{y})^2+(x'_{z})^2}dydz\)
      • \(xOz\)面的投影面积=\(\displaystyle\iint\limits_{D_{xz}}\sqrt{1+(y'_{x})^2+(y'_{z})^2}dxdz\)
    • 一般情况体积=\(\displaystyle\iiint\limits_{\Omega}dv\)

    逆用求多重积分¶

    三重积分¶
    \[\displaystyle\overline{x}=\frac{\displaystyle\iiint\limits_{\Omega}xdv}{\displaystyle\iiint\limits_{\Omega}dv}\Rightarrow\iiint\limits_{\Omega}xdv=\overline{x}\cdot V, V为\Omega的体积$$ ##### 第一型曲线积分 $$\displaystyle\overline{x}=\frac{\displaystyle\int_{L}xds}{\displaystyle\int_{L}ds}\Rightarrow\int_{L}xds=\overline{x}\cdot l_r, l_r为L的长度$$ ##### 第一型曲面积分 $$\displaystyle\overline{x}=\frac{\displaystyle\iint\limits_{\Sigma}xdS}{\displaystyle\iint\limits_{\Sigma}dS}\Rightarrow\iint\limits_{\Sigma}xdS=\overline{x}\cdot S, S为\Sigma的面积\]

    第二型曲线积分¶

    概念¶

    物理意义：变力沿曲线做的总功w=x方向总功+y方向总功(+z方向总功) \(\(\displaystyle W=\int_{L}dW=\int_{L}F(x,y,z)dr=\int_{L}P(x,y,z)dx+Q(x,y,z)dy+R(x,y,z)dz\)\)

    第二型曲面积分¶

    概念¶

    物理意义：向量场总通量=x方向通量+y方向通量+z方向通量 \(\(\displaystyle\iint\limits_{\Sigma}\overrightarrow{F}\cdot d\overrightarrow{S}=\iint\limits_{\Sigma}P(x,y,z)dydz+Q(x,y,z)dzdx+R(d,y,z)dxdy\)\)
